AIC: Akaike Information Criterion (lower is better), for choosing a model

CL: Confidence Limit

df: degrees of freedom

DFr: residual Degrees of Freedom

emmean: Estimated Marginal Mean, aka Least-Squares Mean

GAMLSS: Generalized Additive Model for Location, Scale and Shape

NWEi: Number of Waveform Events by insect

RMSE: Root Mean Squared Error (lower is better), for choosing a model

SE: Standard Error

t_1Pr: time to 1st Probe from the start of the EPG recording

WDi: Waveform Duration by insect

WDEi: Waveform Duration per Event by insect
